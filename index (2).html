The International Classification of Adult Underweight,Overweight and Obesity according to BMI.

Principal cut-off points
Classification BMI(kg/m²)
Underweight <18.50
Servere Tinness <16.00
Moderate Thinness 16.00-16.99
Mild Thinness 17.00-18.49
Normal Range 18.50-24.99
Overweight ≥25.00
Pre-obese 25.00-29.99
Obese ≥30.00
Obese Class I 30.00-34.99
Obese Class II 35.00-39.99
Obese Class III ≥40.00




















N/B:
BMI value are age-independent and the same for both sexes. However, BMI may not correspond to the same degree of fatnesss in different populations due, in part, to different body proportions. The health risks associated with increasing BMI are continous and the interpretation of BMI gradings in relation to risk may differ for diferent populations.
